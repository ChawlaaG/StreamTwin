Privacy Policy for StreamTwin

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

Overview

StreamTwin ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information—specifically regarding the sensitive permissions requested by the Android Operating System.

Data Collection & Permissions

StreamTwin requires the following permissions to function as a live-streaming client:

  • Camera & Microphone: We require access to your device's camera and microphone exclusively to capture audio and video data for your live streams.
  • Foreground Service (Media Projection): We require access to capture your device's screen exclusively to encode your gameplay and broadcast it to your connected streaming platform (Twitch).

How We Use Your Data

  • Live Broadcasting: The audio, video, and screen capture data generated by these permissions is never saved on our servers, analyzed, or shared with third-party advertisers.
  • Direct Transmission: The media data is heavily compressed locally on your device and transmitted directly to the Twitch Ingest Servers using the RTMP protocol via the secure Stream Key you provide.
  • Local VODs: If you enable "Save VOD Locally", the broadcast is saved securely, and exclusively, to your device's local filesystem (Gallery). We do not have access to these files.

Third-Party Services

StreamTwin communicates with Twitch Interactive, Inc. ("Twitch") to authenticate your account and broadcast your stream. By using StreamTwin, you are also bound by the Twitch Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the associated Google Play Store listing.
